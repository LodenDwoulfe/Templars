About Us

The Obsidian Sun Templars are an elite sect of warriors, spellcasters, and devoted acolytes sworn to protect and serve the Sacred Flame, while understanding that the Light and the Sacred Flame are powerful tools that must be wielded with wisdom. Emerging from the deep recesses of Thalassian culture, the Templars are dedicated to shielding Azeroth and advancing the cause of the Sacred Flame wherever they go.

Order Structure

The Emberguard

The Emberguard represents the martial division of the Templars. They are the warriors on the front lines, clad in obsidian armor and wielding weapons blessed by the Sacred Flame. Known for their discipline and unwavering courage, they are often tasked with the most dangerous missions to protect their allies and Azeroth.

The Pyrechanters

The Pyrechanters are the spellcasters and scholars of the order. They serve as the magical backbone, wielding the Sacred Flame to cleanse corruption and defend their allies. They also work tirelessly to uncover more about the history of the flame and enhance the order's magical capabilities.

The Flamewardens

The Flamewardens are the spiritual leaders of the Templars. They tend to rituals, oversee oaths, and provide guidance. Their role is crucial in ensuring every member remains true to the path, offering solace and healing when needed. They are the heart of the order's moral strength.

Events & Roleplay

The Obsidian Sun Templars regularly host a variety of events, including:

  • Sacred Flame Rituals: Weekly ceremonies to honor the Sacred Flame, featuring storytelling, prayers, and offerings.
  • Flame's Trial: Grueling trials to test the courage and faith of new recruits.
  • Cleansing Expeditions: Missions into the darkest corners of Azeroth to root out corruption and void influences.
  • Sunfire Campaigns: Large-scale PvE initiatives where the Templars join forces with other orders to protect the Horde.
