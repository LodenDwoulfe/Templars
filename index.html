Our Divisions

The Emberguard

The Emberguard are the shield and sword of the Templars, the fearless warriors who confront danger head-on. They wear armor forged with sacred symbols, infused with the blessings of the Sacred Flame to enhance their strength and resilience. Their weapons are specially crafted to channel the fiery essence of the Flame, allowing them to incinerate their foes in battle.

Emberguards are known for their relentless training regimens that test their physical limits, as well as their capacity to endure pain and hardship. Their discipline is unmatched, and they embody the will of the Sacred Flame, serving as an unbreakable bastion against the forces of darkness.

The Pyrechanters

The Pyrechanters are the mystical backbone of the Obsidian Sun Templars. These spellcasters dedicate their lives to studying the Sacred Flame's arcane properties, unraveling its mysteries, and discovering new ways to wield its power. They use the Flame to cleanse corruption, heal their allies, and obliterate their enemies. Pyrechanters are responsible for developing the sacred rituals and spells that connect the Templars to the Sacred Flame, as well as teaching new members the ancient incantations that have been passed down through generations.

Pyrechanters are also scholars, delving into ancient texts and tomes to learn about the history of the Flame and the role it has played throughout time. They are dedicated to expanding the order's magical capabilities and preserving the sacred knowledge for future generations.

The Flamewardens

The Flamewardens serve as the spiritual leaders of the Templars. They conduct the sacred rituals, provide moral and spiritual guidance, and maintain the connection between the order and the Sacred Flame. Flamewardens are entrusted with the most important rites, including induction ceremonies, oaths of allegiance, and the blessings bestowed upon weapons and armor.

Flamewardens are also the caretakers of the Sacred Flame itself, ensuring that it is never extinguished and that it continues to burn brightly as a symbol of hope and resilience. They provide emotional support to members who have faced traumatic experiences, using their knowledge of sacred healing to mend both body and spirit.
