About Us - Obsidian Sun Templars

About Us

Who We Are

The Obsidian Sun Templars are a devoted and elite order of warriors, spellcasters, and acolytes committed to the Sacred Flame. They recognize the dual nature of the Light and the Sacred Flame—powerful tools that can bring salvation or destruction. Emerging from ancient Thalassian traditions, the Templars combine their deep spiritual discipline with martial prowess, striving to shield Azeroth from threats and to inspire hope wherever they go.

Founded during a time of great turmoil, the Templars have evolved into an order known for their resolve, integrity, and unwavering loyalty. Their mission is to guard the Sacred Flame, protect the innocent, and wield the Flame as a beacon against the darkness. The Templars adhere to a code that emphasizes the balance between creation and destruction, using their power to ensure that the innocent are protected and that darkness is purged.

Order Structure - Obsidian Sun Templars

Order Structure

Our Divisions

The Emberguard

The Emberguard are the shield and sword of the Templars, the fearless warriors who confront danger head-on. They wear armor forged with sacred symbols, infused with the blessings of the Sacred Flame to enhance their strength and resilience. Their weapons are specially crafted to channel the fiery essence of the Flame, allowing them to incinerate their foes in battle.

Emberguards are known for their relentless training regimens that test their physical limits, as well as their capacity to endure pain and hardship. Their discipline is unmatched, and they embody the will of the Sacred Flame, serving as an unbreakable bastion against the forces of darkness.

The Pyrechanters

The Pyrechanters are the mystical backbone of the Obsidian Sun Templars. These spellcasters dedicate their lives to studying the Sacred Flame's arcane properties, unraveling its mysteries, and discovering new ways to wield its power. They use the Flame to cleanse corruption, heal their allies, and obliterate their enemies. Pyrechanters are responsible for developing the sacred rituals and spells that connect the Templars to the Sacred Flame, as well as teaching new members the ancient incantations that have been passed down through generations.

Pyrechanters are also scholars, delving into ancient texts and tomes to learn about the history of the Flame and the role it has played throughout time. They are dedicated to expanding the order's magical capabilities and preserving the sacred knowledge for future generations.

The Flamewardens

The Flamewardens serve as the spiritual leaders of the Templars. They conduct the sacred rituals, provide moral and spiritual guidance, and maintain the connection between the order and the Sacred Flame. Flamewardens are entrusted with the most important rites, including induction ceremonies, oaths of allegiance, and the blessings bestowed upon weapons and armor.

Flamewardens are also the caretakers of the Sacred Flame itself, ensuring that it is never extinguished and that it continues to burn brightly as a symbol of hope and resilience. They provide emotional support to members who have faced traumatic experiences, using their knowledge of sacred healing to mend both body and spirit.

Events - Obsidian Sun Templars

Events & Roleplay

Events We Host

The Obsidian Sun Templars host a variety of immersive events that bring members together and strengthen their connection to the Sacred Flame. These events include:

  • Sacred Flame Rituals: Weekly ceremonies held to honor the Sacred Flame, where Templars gather for storytelling, prayers, and offerings. These rituals serve to inspire members, remind them of their purpose, and strengthen their bond as an order.
  • Flame's Trial: Grueling trials designed to test the physical and spiritual fortitude of new recruits. These trials push initiates to their limits, challenging them to prove their worthiness to join the ranks of the Templars. The trials often involve tests of combat skill, mental resilience, and unwavering faith in the Sacred Flame.
  • Cleansing Expeditions: Missions into the darkest corners of Azeroth to locate and eradicate void influences, corrupted beings, and other threats. Cleansing Expeditions are dangerous endeavors that require the full strength of the Templars working in unison to confront the darkness and ensure its defeat.
  • Sunfire Campaigns: Large-scale PvE initiatives where the Templars join forces with other allied orders to protect the Horde and bring light to areas threatened by malevolent forces. These campaigns are opportunities for the Templars to showcase their unity, discipline, and strength in battle.
